About Me

My name is Courtney Dowd. I have been in the animal care industry since 2003. I started out as a doggie daycare attendant, then attended grooming school. I worked in the boarding and grooming industry until 2006, until I decided to pursue an education in veterinary technology. In 2008, I became a licensed veterinary technician. I currently work part time as an LVT. I have also been pet sitting for the last ten years. My belief is that all pets should be treated as though they were my own. References are available upon request.

Services

Pet Sitting / Dog Walking

Pets often do best in their own home environment. A visit includes any medication administration, litter box scooping, feeding, playtime, and walks if needed. I will also provide you with daily updates and pictures of your pet! A visit typically last up to 30 minutes, depending on the individual needs of the pet.

  • $29.50 per visit* (up to 30 minutes)
  • $36 (45 minute visit)

*$5 extra charge per visit for holidays (New Years Day, Memorial Day, 4th of July, Labor Day, Thanksgiving, Christmas)

*Clients outside the base service area will be subject to a $3 per visit charge to cover extra mileage.
